About

Bayesian Case Competition is a case competition on financial risk management where 12-15 teams of contestants compete to build a developed solution for an open-ended case on market and investment risk mitigation to be presented to a panel of judges. This year's prize pool will consist of $3,000+ in cash prizes and other gift prizes. Bayesian 2018 will take place on January 27th at the University of Waterloo.

Timeline

Registration Open

Registration opens for all delegates for the Bayesian Case Competition. Delegates can register below as a team of 3-4 or as an individual competitor. Individual delegates will be matched 1 day prior to case release.

Team Individual January 1, 2018

Information Session

Held on campus at the University of Waterloo, this session serves as an introduction to the ideas behind Bayesian, to answer any questions from prospective delegates concerning the competition. Keep an eye out for more details posted on the Bayesian and FARMSA Facebook pages.

January 9, 2018

Registration Closes

General registration for delegates closes at 11:59 PM EST. It is highly recommended for anyone interested in participating to register early. Delegates or teams registering after this date and prior to Friday, Jan 19th will be admitted at the discretion of the Bayesian planning committee.

January 12, 2018

Case Released

At 11:59 AM EST, the case will be released to all registered teams. All teams will have 12 days to create a case solution and presentation. For late registrants the case will be distributed shortly after confirmation of your attendance.

January 13, 2018

Submission Deadline

Submission of any case-related content is due at 11:59 PM EST. No submissions or changes to presentations will be permitted after this deadline. Cases will be vetted and qualifying teams will be notified on Friday, Jan 26th and invited to present their case solution to a panel of judges.

January 24, 2018

Case Competition

The Bayesian Case Competition will take place at STC at the University of Waterloo, from 8:30 AM to 7:00 PM. The day will be jam packed with competition, workshops, panels, networking sessions, and a networking dinner with our esteemed judges and sponsors. Lunch and dinner will be served. A full schedule will be posted closer to the competition date. To all delegates, good luck and happy pitching!
